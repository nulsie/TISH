The Sauce.

Because nobody likes reading docs. But you're here, so let's make it quick, painless, and a little TISH-y. Here is how to use TISH without losing your mind.

What is TISH?

Let’s be real: looking up vulnerabilities usually feels like reading a tax return. TISH (btw it stands for Threat Intelligence, Served Hot) was built to change that. I grab the scariest data on the web and serve it in a way that actually makes sense to human beings. Best part? Everything like your watchlists and webhooks is saved locally in your browser's localStorage. No creepy tracking, just pure threat intel. Whether you're a dev checking your packages or a researcher hunting zero-days, I’ve got your back.

What can it actually do?

CVE Deep Dives

We pull directly from the NVD (NIST) database but add the "spicy" bits: EPSS exploit probabilities and CISA's "Known Exploited" warnings. I'll also scour the GitHub API to find you community Proof of Concepts (PoCs).

SBOM Scanning

Paste your package.json or requirements.txt. I hit the OSV.dev API to tell you exactly which dependency is about to ruin your weekend and provide a text-based. I'll sometimes even suggest exact version updates to fix the mess.

Malware/Actor Search

Not every threat is a CVE. I query a curated malware/actor databank via the MITRE ATT&CK malware and threat actor database to give you the backstories on the world's nastiest digital bugs and the people who did it.

Alerts

Hit the "Alerts" button in the nav. Drop your Discord webhook URL in there. Now, whenever the Live Threat Feed catches a new CVE or something on your Watchlist updates, TISH will slide right into your Discord channel with a styled alert.

Under the Hood

I didn't reinvent the wheel—I just polished it. TISH runs on some incredible open-source tech and public APIs:

  • NVD API 2.0 — The source of truth for vulnerabilities.
  • FIRST.org EPSS — The crystal ball for exploit prediction.
  • ️OSV.dev — Google’s(i know it's Google but this was the best i could find) open-source vulnerability distributed database.
  • Geist & Geist Mono — The sexiest fonts known to mankind.
  • DOMPurify — Keeping your browser safe from nasty XSS injections.
